# NMAP CHEATSHEET

1. Dasar Nmap

---

Scan host dengan default:
nmap <target>

Scan beberapa host:
nmap 192.168.1.1 192.168.1.2
nmap 192.168.1.1-50
nmap 192.168.1.0/24

2. Scan Port

---

Scan 1000 port default:
nmap <target>

Scan port tertentu:
nmap -p 80 <target>

Scan range port:
nmap -p 1-1000 <target>

Scan semua port:
nmap -p- <target>

3. Scan Service & Version

---

nmap -sV <target>
nmap -p 21,22,80 -sV <target>

4. Scan OS Detection

---

nmap -O <target>
nmap -A <target>        (OS detection, version, script, traceroute)

5. Scan Stealth Mode

---

SYN scan (default root):
nmap -sS <target>

UDP scan:
nmap -sU <target>

TCP connect scan:
nmap -sT <target>

FIN scan (bypass firewall):
nmap -sF <target>

6. Aggressive Scan

---

nmap -A <target>
(Meliputi OS detection, service version, script scanning, traceroute)

7. Script Scanning (NSE)

---

Gunakan script bawaan:
nmap --script=default <target>

Gunakan kategori vuln:
nmap --script=vuln <target>

Gunakan script spesifik:
nmap --script=http-title <target>
nmap --script=ftp-anon <target>

8. Timing dan Evasion

---

Atur speed scan (0-5):
nmap -T4 <target>

Fragmentasi paket:
nmap -f <target>

Gunakan decoy:
nmap -D RND:10 <target>

9. Output Nmap

---

Output ke file:
nmap -oN hasil.txt <target>
nmap -oX hasil.xml <target>
nmap -oG hasil.grep <target>

10. Contoh Workflow

---

1. Quick scan host alive:
   nmap -sn 192.168.1.0/24

2. Scan port terbuka:
   nmap -p- -T4 <target>

3. Scan service version:
   nmap -sV -sC <target>

4. Aggressive scan detail:
   nmap -A <target>

5. Tips Penting

---

* Gunakan -sC untuk script default.
* Gunakan -Pn jika ping di-block oleh firewall.
* Mulai dari scan cepat (-T4 -F) lalu lanjut detail.
* Simpan hasil scan ke file untuk dokumentasi.
